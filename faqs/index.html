Preparation

What if I have allergies or can’t do everything on the itinerary?

We will do our best to provide the best accommodation for all our participants. When booking, please provide any notes that we need to consider before departing.

I don’t live in Shanghai but I still want to join?

Currently, we are based in Shanghai but constantly working to provide other great travel experiences throughout China.

Reservation

What’s your cancellation policy?

In case of cancellation, the deposit is not refundable and cannot be used for another trip unless the replacement has been provided.

Do you provide private trips?

Yes, we organize private trips for small and big groups as well as team building and all kinds of events. Please contact us to learn more

What accommodations are provided?

Each trip has it’s own personalized accommodation list ranging from hotels rooms to private shuttles. We will provide a detail intinerary after a booking reservation is confirmed.

How can I book a trip?

You can now buy a trip directly from our website via Paypal or major credit card payment.  Alternatively, please fill out the contact form within the site or contact us via Wechat, email, or by phone. Secondly, we will need a 500 RMB deposit to reserve your place. We accept payment via Paypal, Wechat payment, bank transfer, or cash payment in our Jing’an office.

Weather

What if the weather is poor?

If the weather prevents us from continuing our trip, we will send an announcement with the proceeding steps.
